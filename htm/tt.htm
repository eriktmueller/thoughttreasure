Spec Sheet

ThoughtTreasure brings natural language and commonsense capabilities to computers and devices:

  • Its database of 25,000 concepts, 55,000 English and French words and phrases, 50,000 assertions, and 100 scripts makes the human world more understandable to computers.
  • Its natural language parser and generator allow computers to extract information from text and answer questions.

Sample items in ThoughtTreasure are:

Dogs bark.
Soda is a drink.
People have necks.
Gold hair is called blond hair.
A play lasts about two hours.
One hangs up at the end of a phone call.
